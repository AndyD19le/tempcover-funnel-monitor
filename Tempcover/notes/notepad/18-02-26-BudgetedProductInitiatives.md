# Summary: Budgeted Product Initiatives
**Document Creation Date:** February 18, 2026
**Source:** Budgeted Product Initiatives Slide Deck

---

## 1. Key Performance Sensitivities
* **Conversion Value**: A 1 percentage point (1pp) increase in conversion is worth approximately **£0.24 million per month**.
* **Customer Retention Value**: An increase of 0.1 policies per customer is worth approximately **£113,000 in revenue per month**.

---

## 2. Strategic Initiatives

### **Strategy 1: Retention & Returning Customers**
* **Goal**: Increase policies per customer from **2.6 to 3.3 by 2030**.
* **Upcoming Features (Early 2026)**: Launching "Returning Customer" experiences, including:
    * User accounts.
    * Quote pre-fill.
    * 1-click extend and rebuy functionality.

### **Strategy 2: App Migration**
* **Goal**: Shift customers away from high-cost **Paid Search** toward the **Mobile App**.
* **Approach**: Use targeted discounting, unique app features, and push notifications to stay "front of mind at the point of need".
* **Impact**: Aiming to move roughly **5% of policies** out of Paid Search and into the app to improve LTV.

### **Strategy 3: Market Expansion (Learner Drivers)**
* **Market Opportunity**: 3.7 million UK learner drivers annually; 76% practice privately.
* **The Product**: A market-leading **£10/hour fixed-price Learner product**.
* **Growth Target**: Scale TAM share from **5% in 2027 to 16.5% by 2030**.

### **Strategy 4: Monthly Subscription Product**
* **Goal**: Target "Annual Replacers" (customers buying multiple 28-day policies).
* **The Product**: A flexible, **auto-renewing subscription** with no tie-ins.
* **2030 Projections**: £11m incremental revenue and ~50,000 incremental customers.
