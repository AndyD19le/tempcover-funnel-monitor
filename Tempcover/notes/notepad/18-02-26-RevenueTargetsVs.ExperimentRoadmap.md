# Strategic Cross-Reference: Revenue Targets vs. Experiment Roadmap
**Document Creation Date:** February 18, 2026
**Context:** Mapping Onboarding Meeting Notes to Long-term Strategic Goals

---

## 1. Goal: Increase Customer Retention (2.6 to 3.3 Policies)
**Financial Sensitivity**: +0.1 policies per customer = **~£113k revenue/month**.

| Relevant Experiments | Strategic Alignment |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"One-Click" Rebuy/Extend** | Directly reduces friction for repeat purchases. |
| **User Accounts & Pre-fill** | Required foundation to recognize returning users. |
| **Deep Linking for Expiry** | Uses push notifications to capture users at the moment of policy expiration. |

---

## 2. Goal: App Migration (Shift 5% of Policies from Paid Search)
**Strategic Context**: Paid search is only profitable after the **6th policy**; App LTV is superior.

| Relevant Experiments | Strategic Alignment |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"App in Your Pocket" Strategy** | Direct engagement to bypass Google search costs. |
| **App-Exclusive Discounting** | Financial incentive for initial app download/account creation. |
| **Unified UX (Authentication)** | Seamless transition between Web and App to reduce drop-offs during migration. |

---

## 3. Goal: Conversion Rate Optimization (CVR)
**Financial Sensitivity**: +1 percentage point (1pp) CVR = **~£240,000 revenue/month**.

| Relevant Experiments | Strategic Alignment |
| :--- | :--- |
| **One Question per Page** | Targeting a significant CVR uplift using GDS/Gov.uk standards. |
| **Remove License Duration** | Removing technically redundant questions to shorten the funnel. |
| **Summarize Quote at Payment** | Increasing checkout confidence to reduce payment-screen abandonment. |
| **Defaulting Popular Durations** | Reducing clicks by pre-selecting common choices (e.g., 2 hours). |

---
