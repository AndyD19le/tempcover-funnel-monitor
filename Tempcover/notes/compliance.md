# RVU Regulatory & Compliance Master Reference

## 1. Overview & Brands
RVU operates as a bridge between consumers and financial/essential services.
**Brands in Scope:** Confused.com, Mojo Mortgages, Money, Tempcover, and Uswitch.
**Primary Jurisdiction:** UK (FCA, CMA, ASA, ICO).

## 2. The FCA Consumer Duty (The High Standard)
Firms must prioritize customer interests and act to deliver **good outcomes** for retail customers.

### Cross-Cutting Rules
* **Act in Good Faith:** Be honest and transparent (e.g., SEO content must be easily understood).
* **Avoid Foreseeable Harm:** Proactively fix issues (e.g., updating a journey based on negative consumer feedback).
* **Enable & Support:** Empower consumers to meet financial goals (e.g., providing the right information at the right time).

### The Four Outcomes
1. **Products & Services:** Designed for a specific target market and reviewed regularly to remain relevant.
2. **Price & Value:** Must demonstrate a fair relationship between price and benefits.
3. **Consumer Understanding:** Information must be clear; risks and benefits must have equal prominence.
4. **Consumer Support:** Support must be accessible and effective throughout the product lifecycle.

## 3. FCA Individual Conduct Rules
Mandatory behavioral standards for almost all RVU employees:
1. **Integrity:** Admitting mistakes (e.g., pricing errors) and ensuring accurate financial reporting.
2. **Due Skill, Care, & Diligence:** Following all due diligence and onboarding processes.
3. **Cooperation:** Being open and honest with the FCA and other regulators.
4. **Customer Interests:** Prioritizing fairness over commercial gain in strategic meetings.
5. **Market Conduct:** Observing high standards of professional behavior.
6. **Good Outcomes:** Ensuring the final result for the customer is positive and offers fair value.

## 4. CMA CARE Principles & Consumer Law
Applies specifically to comparison and switching services:
* **Clear:** Transparent about market coverage and how results are ranked/ordered.
* **Accurate:** No misleading data or omissions of material information.
* **Responsible:** Acting in the user's best interest during the journey.
* **Easy to Use:** Intuitive design that does not utilize "sludge" or aggressive nudges.

### Unfair Commercial Practices (DMCC Act)
* **No Drip Pricing:** Total costs (fees, taxes, upfront costs) must be shown upfront.
* **Transparency:** Mid-contract price rises must be clearly disclosed.

## 5. Risk Management & Events
**Risk Event:** Any unforeseen disruption (process failure, tech outage, or partner security incident).
**Action:** Must be logged immediately via Slack using `/inc` or `/incident` (set type to **Risk Event**).

## 6. Mandatory Compliance Sign-Off
You **must** obtain formal sign-off via **Jira** for:
* **UX/UI Changes:** Ranking, filtering, question sets, or homepage layouts.
* **Marketing:** SEO content, PR, Ads (TV/Social), E-CRM, and weekly emails.
* **New Products:** Entry into new product areas or new products from existing partners.
* **Claims:** Any claim involving savings or "market-leading" status (requires ASA substantiation).

## 7. Consequences of Non-Compliance
* **Individual:** Disciplinary action reportable to the regulator; personal fines.
* **Corporate:** Fines up to **10% of annual global turnover** (CMA).
* **Operational:** Withdrawal of regulatory permissions (removing products from websites).
* **Reputational:** Loss of consumer trust and partner relationships.
