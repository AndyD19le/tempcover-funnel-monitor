# RVU Training Reference: Conflicts of Interest & Whistleblowing

## 1. Conflict of Interest (CoI) Reference
### Definitions
* **Actual:** A direct conflict between professional duty and personal interest.
* **Potential:** A situation that could become a conflict in the future.
* **Perceived:** A situation where an observer could reasonably think a conflict exists.

### Core Principles (FCA)
* **Principle 1 (Integrity):** Conduct business with honesty.
* **Principle 6 (Customers' Interests):** Treat customers fairly.
* **Principle 8 (Conflicts):** Manage conflicts fairly between the firm and customers.

---

## 2. Gifts & Entertainment (G&E) Policy
All G&E must be **Appropriate, Business-Related, Reasonable, Open, Occasional,** and **In-Kind Only** (strictly no cash or vouchers).

### Approval Thresholds
| Value | Action Required | Primary Approver |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Any Value** | Advance Written Approval | Line Manager |
| **> £50** | Record in RVU G&E Register | Line Manager |
| **> £200** | "Expensive/Unusual" Approval | Dept. Lead & Legal Team |
| **Tenders** | Negotiation Period Restriction | Senior Legal & Director |

*Note: Records must be kept for at least 10 years.*

## 3. Whistleblowing & Reporting
### The Duty to Report
**Anyone** at RVU or acting on RVU’s behalf has a duty to report wrongdoing.
* **Reportable items:** Bribery, Fraud, H&S breaches, Bullying, Environmental damage, or concealment of these acts.
* **Protection:** You are protected from retaliation or job loss for raising a genuine concern.

### Reporting Channels
1.  **Internal:** Line Manager, HR (`hr@rvu.co.uk`), or Legal (`legal@rvu.co.uk`).

## 4. Training Quiz: Correct Actions
* **In Doubt?** Seek guidance from Manager, HR, or Legal.
* **Hiring a friend/relative?** Remove yourself from the recruitment process entirely.
* **Vendor is a relative?** Disclose the connection and ensure a standard procurement process.
* **Retribution?** No, RVU prohibits retaliation for whistleblowing.
* **Approval for >£200?** Requires Departmental Lead and Legal Team sign-off.
